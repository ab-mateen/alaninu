FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Alani Rewards?

Alanis rewards program, gives you the opportunity to score store credit, access exclusives, and more! As a member, youll earn points for every purchase and for engaging with our brand through social media and other platforms. Once you earn enough points to unlock rewards, points can be redeemed like cash at check out!

Do points expire?

Nope, once you earn points, they are yours for good!

How do I join Alani rewards?

If you have an account, youre already a member of Alanis rewards! If not, click Join Now'.

How do I earn points?

Many ways! Youll earn points just by joining Alani Rewards. After that, points can be earned with each purchase, as well as sharing and following us on social media. See our full list of ways to earn above!

How do I check my points balance?

Log into your account and visit the rewards tab for a summary of all of your points!

What happens to my points when I return items?

When items are refunded, the points you earned from that purchase will be deducted from your point balance.

How do I spend my points?

Once youre ready to check out, open your cart by clicking the shopping bag in the top right corner of our site to view your points balance and the redemption options. Select the points that youd like to apply to your order and continue to checkout! You can add as many rewards as your balance permits.
